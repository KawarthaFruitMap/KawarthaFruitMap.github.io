Welcome to the Kawartha and Haliburton Community Fruit Tree and Vegetable Harvesting and sharing program

This exciting new rural Ontario pilot project program has been created and supported by a group of agencies and government that include Hiawatha First Nation, The City of Kawartha Lakes, Haliburton County City of Kawartha Lakes Roundtable for Ending Poverty, the Kawartha Lakes Food Coalition Food Security Working Group and Fleming College Urban Forestry and GIS departments.

Kawartha Lakes and Haliburton County are on the territory of the Michi Saagiig Anishnaabe who have cared for these lands since time immemorial and are covered by Treaty 20 and the Williams Treaty.
